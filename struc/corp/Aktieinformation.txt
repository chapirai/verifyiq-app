* aktiekapital -> shareCapital
* aktiekapital.belopp -> shareCapital.amount
* aktiekapital.valuta -> shareCapital.currency
* antalAktier -> totalShares
* aktiegranser -> shareLimits

* aktiegranser.aktiekapitalGranser.lagst -> shareLimits.capitalLimits.min

* kvotvarde.valuta -> quotaValue.currency

Rules:

* aktiekapital.belopp and kvotvarde.belopp: decimal number, preserve precision
* antalAktier and aktieslag.antal: int64, must be handled without precision loss
* aktieslagGranser may be missing or empty
* valuta fields: allowed values SEK or EUR
* rostvarde: string, preserve as-is
* fritext: free text, do not trim or parse
* optional/missing nested objects should be null or omitted, not defaulted
